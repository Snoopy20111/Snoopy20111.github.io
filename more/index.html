Reaper Scripts

A repository of Reapack compliant Lua scripts used for Reaper. Mostly simple utilities.
If you're interested and have Reapack, go to Extensions > Reapack > Import Repositories... and paste the following:

https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Snoopy20111/snoop-ReaScripts/main/index.xml

Region Utility

Making Regions less tedious.

Currently makes a region for every selected media item, named after and assigned to its track. Further plans include adding a GUI, more options for names and region matrix assignments, and also better naming/numbering.

Snap End of Media Items

Moves selected media items so their ends meet the nearest grid-line.

Pairs well with scripts that don't cut media items on gridlines. Makes it much easier to edit, make regions over, etc.

Region Prefix/Suffix

For adjusting Region names en masse.

Largely edited from Acendan's region enumeration script, and similar to the ReaPack standards Find And Replace in Regions/Markers (also written by Acendan). Please go look at his scripts and other work, especially with the UCS.

Solar Science

Kerbal Space Program Mod.
Praise the Sun!

This was a project I originally undertook during my senior year of high school as a directed study. I modeled, textured, animated, and scripted two new experiment parts, based upon those aboard NASA's real SOHO and STEREO missions. Over the years I made small additions, such as experiment requirements, custom sfx, and loading real-time images from NASA's feeds when the experiment is run. As KSP is made in Unity, I used it and C# extensively.

Over the 5 years I maintained and expanded the mod, Solar Science managed somewhere around almost 50k downloads across all platforms. In early 2020 I handed the project off to KSP Forums user zer0Kerbal, who has maintained the mod since.
